# Curriculum Manager — Getting Started

A quick guide for testing the new **Curriculum Manager** in Peer Support Studio. It lets your organization set up structured programs (like PND, Living in Balance, or your own), enroll participants, log group sessions with attendance, and automatically track each person's progress toward completion.

> **What it is:** the skeleton and tracking for a program — modules, enrollments, attendance, and completion. It holds the *structure*, not copyrighted curriculum content.

---

## 1. Finding it

You can open the module two ways:

- **Top navigation:** click **Curricula**.
- **Dashboard:** the **Curriculum Manager** card (graduation-cap icon).

Both land you on the curriculum list.

---

## 2. Create a curriculum

From the list page, click **Create Curriculum**. You have two options:

1. **Quick Template** — click the **PND** template to pre-fill a ready-made 16-module program (Portal New Direction). You can edit anything before saving.
2. **Manual Build** — fill in the details yourself:
   - **Name**, **description**, and **source** (e.g. "Hazelden", "State DOC", "Internal")
   - **Total hours** estimate (optional)
   - **Modules** — add as many as you need. Each has a number, title, an optional minimum time, and an optional description.

Click **Save** and you'll land on the new curriculum's detail page.

---

## 3. The curriculum detail page

Four tabs:

| Tab | What it's for |
| --- | --- |
| **Overview** | The curriculum's description and the full numbered module list. |
| **Enrollments** | The participants in this program and their progress. |
| **Sessions** | The group sessions you've delivered, with attendance. |
| **Reports** | Coming soon (completion reports, attendance summaries, certificates). |

---

## 4. Enroll participants

1. Open the **Enrollments** tab.
2. Click **Enroll Participant**.
3. Search and check one or more participants, then click **Enroll**.

Each enrolled participant shows a **progress bar** (modules completed / total) and a **status** dropdown — Active, Completed, Paused, or Withdrawn. You can change status at any time.

> Progress starts at 0% — it fills in as you log sessions (next step).

---

## 5. Log a session (the main workflow)

This is how attendance and progress get recorded.

1. Go to the **Sessions** tab and click **Log Session**.
2. Fill in:
   - **Module** delivered
   - **Date** (defaults to today)
   - **Duration** in minutes (optional)
   - **Location** (optional, if your org has locations set up)
   - **Attendance** — everyone with an active enrollment is listed and defaults to **Present**. Tap a person's status chip to cycle it: Present → Absent → Excused → Late.
   - **Notes** (optional)
3. Click **Save Session**.

**What happens automatically:** every participant marked **Present** gets that module marked complete, and their progress bar moves. When someone completes *all* modules, their enrollment flips to **Completed** on its own.

---

## 6. View or edit a logged session

On the **Sessions** tab, **click any session row** to open its detail. You can:

- Adjust the date, duration, location, or notes
- Re-take attendance (tap the status chips)
- Click **Save Changes**

The trash icon on a session row deletes that session.

> **Good to know — completions are "sticky":** marking someone **Present** (when logging or editing) credits them with that module. Switching them *away* from Present later does **not** remove an already-earned completion, and deleting a session keeps completions too. This is intentional — a completion means the work was done. If you log someone present by mistake, let us know how you'd want that corrected.

---

## 7. See a participant's curricula

Open any participant's detail page and click the **Curricula** tab to see every program they're enrolled in, with a progress bar for each. Handy for a quick "where is this person at?" check.

---

## What to test & report

Please try these and note anything confusing or broken:

- [ ] Create a curriculum both ways (PND template + manual)
- [ ] Enroll several participants (try the multi-select)
- [ ] Log a session and confirm progress bars move for present attendees
- [ ] Mark someone Absent/Excused and confirm they're **not** credited
- [ ] Complete every module for one participant — confirm their enrollment auto-flips to **Completed**
- [ ] Click into a logged session, change attendance, and save
- [ ] Check the participant's **Curricula** tab matches what you see on the curriculum page

**Known / by design for this phase:**
- The **Reports** tab is a placeholder ("coming soon").
- There's no certificate generation yet.

**Reporting feedback:** send notes to your admin contact with the curriculum name, what you did, what you expected, and what happened (a screenshot helps).

Thanks for testing!
